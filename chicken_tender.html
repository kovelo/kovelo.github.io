Chicken Tender

About

Chicken tender solves the "What do you want to eat?" fight forever.

Play with your friends to finally decide on where to eat.

Nearby Restaurants

Chicken Tender automatically finds all nearby restaurants for you and your friends to play with.

Slide for Food

Each restaurant is displayed as a card that can be slided left or right.

See Likes

When you have acted on all nearby restaurants, see all liked restaurants amongst your friends. Only thing left to do is eat!
